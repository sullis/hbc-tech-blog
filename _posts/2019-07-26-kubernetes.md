At HBC, our production applications run in Docker containers on a public cloud. Kubernetes has quickly emerged as a core building block for distributed systems. All major cloud platforms (AWS, Google, Microsoft) now offer Kubernetes-as-a-service. Our engineering team is excited about the potential of Kubernetes for our production environment.

Here is a list of presentations about Kubernetes that we have found educational.
